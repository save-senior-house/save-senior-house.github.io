In Senior House, a common study break and community building activity is to barbeque in the courtyard with some friends. The BBQ pit is not only a part of Senior House culture, but a practical amenity to have for a building, yet the administration found it to be wise to quietly rip it out of the ground.

The tire swing was adorned to the tree in the Senior House courtyard by an alumnus resident of the House a long time ago. Residents through the ages has swung on this tire, and now it has been disrespectfully cut down, with no rhyme or reason.
